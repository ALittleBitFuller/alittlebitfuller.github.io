About

I am a designer in a developer’s body, or vice versa. Basically, I just love to build stuff! And my brain has the rare ability to work equally well on both sides, which helps me to appreciate both form and function. That ability also gives me with the rare capacity to bridge the communication gap between the technical and non-technical among us, and as my career progresses I hope that skill will aid me in providing leadership that demonstrates a passion for creativity that is both motivating and contagious.

Vision

I have over 18 years of experience building rich, robust applications, and I am published on the Apple Store and the Google Play Store. I also have over 18 years of experience in design, development, budget analysis, and project management, fortified by excellent written and oral communication skills. My desire is to move more into the front-end and mobile development and design spaces, while continuing to maintain my server side skills as well. And eventually, I hope to have the opportunity to explore leadership roles that still provide outlets for creativity. As a relentless self-learner who thrives on engaging new technologies and has a reputation for creativity, trustworthiness, judicious management of priorities, and a work ethic that goes the extra mile, I am always up for a new challenge.
